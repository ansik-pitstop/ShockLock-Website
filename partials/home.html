ShockLock is all about SAFETY

ShockLock is the strongest solution for failed gas struts. It is a heavy duty tool to help hold up hatches like hoods and trunks, providing a safe working environment for technicians and vehicle owners. Intuitively designed, ShockLock reduces vehicle damage liability, while optimizing engine workspace.
One Handed Application

ShockLock is easy to use and you can install/uninstall it with only one hand.

Supports 100 lbs of Weight

ShockLock is strong! ShockLock supports 100 lbs of weight while the average hatch is 40lbs.

Non Damaging

ShockLock is made from 100% non-damaging aluminium.
